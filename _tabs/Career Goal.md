# My Future Career

Hello everyone.  
Today, I will talk about my future career.

When I was young, I wanted to become an engineer because I really liked robots.

Later, I learned that engineering is closely related to computer science.  
At that time, AI became a very popular topic, so I naturally became interested in AI development.

Now, I am studying Artificial Intelligence at university and learning the basic skills of AI and programming.

After university, I want to work as an AI researcher at Samsung.  
Especially, I want to work in the AI development department in Suwon, Gyeonggi-do.

After working for some time, I want to try something different.  
I heard that many developers in Silicon Valley write poetry after retirement, and I also want to write poems someday.

There are many other things I want to learn too.  
I want to gain experience in many industries such as automobiles, healthcare, and app development because developers need many different user experiences.

Thank you for listening.
